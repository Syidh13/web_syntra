Key modules

A practical set of modules that cover daily operations end-to-end. Replace images with your real screenshots later.

Asset Monitoring

Live visibility for vehicle status, device health, and operational readiness across fleets.

  • • Fleet availability and status (active, standby, breakdown)
  • • Device connectivity and data freshness indicators
  • • Maintenance flags, downtime trends, and utilization

Headway Management

Keep service stable by monitoring gaps and bunching, with clear recommendations for recovery actions.

  • • Detect bunching, gaps, and abnormal dwell time
  • • Dispatch suggestions: hold, short-turn, add/remove unit
  • • Corridor-level heat view and incident overlays

Mileage Verification

Verification layer to reconcile planned trips vs actual movements, useful for SLA and billing integrity.

  • • Validate trips, mileage, and route compliance
  • • Flag anomalies: detours, missing trips, GPS gaps
  • • Export-ready evidence for audit and reporting

Scheduling

Plan service across time blocks, fleets, and corridors with constraints and scenario comparison.

  • • Timetable planning with constraints and capacity
  • • Scenario compare: demand shift, event days, disruptions
  • • Export for GTFS or operational deployment

Driver Performance

Performance signals for safety, compliance, and operational quality. Useful for coaching and accountability.

  • • Driving behavior, compliance, incident history
  • • On-time adherence and operational discipline
  • • Ranking, coaching notes, and improvement tracking

Incident & SLA

Track incidents, response times, and SLA outcomes with clear visibility across operators and corridors.

  • • Incident timeline and operational actions
  • • SLA dashboards with drill-down evidence
  • • Reporting exports for stakeholders
